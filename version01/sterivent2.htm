SteriVent - The principle function

Sterile filter - a new approach, a different and cheap solution

The new sterile filter, “Sterivent” solves a whole complex of the following problems:
1) Sterility, longer shelf life of the product – by means of simple process, in low coast.
2) The filter enables up - grade of every existing tank to sterile tank.
The innovation is in creation of sterility in the tank and not in his surroundings.
3) Prevention of implosion of the tank:
Every tank has an automatic system of cleaning (CIP). In the process of cleaning, after the hot phase comes the cold phase.
