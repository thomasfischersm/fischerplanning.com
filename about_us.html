About Fischer Planning

Fischer Planning, www.fischerplanning.com, is a consulting and manufacturing company specializing in the expert planning, design and construction of sanitary systems for food, beverage, cosmetic and pharmaceutical processing.

Fischer Planning developed and markets the breakthrough SteriVent high-efficiency sterile air filtration system, which protects tanks and products from airborne contaminants. SteriVent offers manufacturers the multiple benefits of longer product shelf life, CIP support and lower energy costs. Using SteriVent can upgrade every tank to an ultra clean tank. All Fischer Planning products are manufactured to our specifications by an SI ISO 9001 “Quality Assured Firm” as recognized by The Standards Institute of Israel.

Friedrich Fischer is founder and president of Fischer Planning.
