Sample Diagrams

Block Diagram
The Block Diagram provide for each project includes all the necessary process steps, production equipment and energy requirements. Different colors indicate new and future equipment. The Block Diagram is the basis for the Flow Diagram.

Flow Diagram
The Flow Diagram provided for each project is a more detailed version of the Block Diagram. The Flow Diagram includes all the necessary production equipment with details such as the specific sizes and quantities of tanks, piping, and the heating and CIP systems. Different colors indicate new and future equipment. The Flow Diagram is the master plan of the project, and serves as the basis for the Layout and the cost estimate.

Layout and Estimating
The Layout includes architectural specifications such as the sizes of building elements, as well as all the necessary equipment. The Layout is used by the architect as the basis for design and planning. At this point a full budget estimate and timetable can be generated, including construction, equipment, materials, energy and other costs.
